\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a unit within a city that has a capacity and tax rate. 

Abstract base class representing a unit within the city, such as buildings or districts.

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classCityUnit}{City\+Unit}} class manages the total and used capacities and the tax rate associated with a city unit.
