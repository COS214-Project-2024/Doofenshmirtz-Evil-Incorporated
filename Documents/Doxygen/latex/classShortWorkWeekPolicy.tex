\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Command to implement a short workweek policy in a \mbox{\hyperlink{classCityUnit}{City\+Unit}}. 

This policy allows for the enhancement of the workweek structure by deducting a certain amount from the balance and increasing the short week policy multiplier.
