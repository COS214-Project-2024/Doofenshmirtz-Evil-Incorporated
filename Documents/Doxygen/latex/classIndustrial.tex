\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents an industrial building in the city simulation. 

Derived class representing an industrial building in the city simulation.

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classIndustrial}{Industrial}} class inherits from \mbox{\hyperlink{classBuilding}{Building}} and includes functionality specific to industrial buildings, such as resource collection and capacity management.

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classIndustrial}{Industrial}} class extends the \mbox{\hyperlink{classBuilding}{Building}} base class, adding functionality specific to industrial buildings, such as resource collection and updates to capacity over time.
