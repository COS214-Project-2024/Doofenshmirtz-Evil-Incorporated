\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Concrete state representing a citizen\textquotesingle{}s location when they are at work. 

This class defines the behavior of a citizen when they are in their work state. It inherits from \mbox{\hyperlink{classCitizenLocationState}{Citizen\+Location\+State}} and provides specific travel behavior when transitioning from work to other locations.
