\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a power plant utility in the city simulation. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classPower}{Power}} class inherits from the \mbox{\hyperlink{classUtility}{Utility}} class and provides functionality to manage its total and used capacity, collect resources, and provide utility data specific to power plants.
