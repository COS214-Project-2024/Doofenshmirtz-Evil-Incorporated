\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a landmark building in the city simulation. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classLandmark}{Landmark}} class inherits from the \mbox{\hyperlink{classBuilding}{Building}} class and provides functionality to manage its total and used capacity, collect resources, and provide utility data specific to landmarks.
