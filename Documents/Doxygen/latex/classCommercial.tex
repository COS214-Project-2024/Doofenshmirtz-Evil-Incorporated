\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a commercial building in the city simulation. 

Represents a commercial building in the city.

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classCommercial}{Commercial}} class inherits from \mbox{\hyperlink{classBuilding}{Building}} and manages resources, salary payments to residents, and notifications for front-\/end updates.

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classCommercial}{Commercial}} class inherits from the \mbox{\hyperlink{classBuilding}{Building}} class and implements functionalities specific to commercial buildings, such as updating salaries for residents, collecting resources, and providing JSON representation.
