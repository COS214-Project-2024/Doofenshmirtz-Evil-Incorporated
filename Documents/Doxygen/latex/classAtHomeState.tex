\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Concrete state representing a citizen\textquotesingle{}s location when they are at home. 

This class defines the behavior of a citizen when they are in their home state. It inherits from \mbox{\hyperlink{classCitizenLocationState}{Citizen\+Location\+State}} and provides a specific implementation for the travel function.

Handles the travel logic for a citizen in the \mbox{\hyperlink{classAtHomeState}{At\+Home\+State}}. 

Transitions the citizen from the \mbox{\hyperlink{classAtHomeState}{At\+Home\+State}} to the \mbox{\hyperlink{classAtWorkState}{At\+Work\+State}}, if the citizen has a job. If the citizen is unemployed, they remain in the \mbox{\hyperlink{classAtHomeState}{At\+Home\+State}}.


\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}

\hline
\end{DoxyParams}
This method transitions a citizen from their home to their workplace, provided they are employed (i.\+e., they have a job). The method calculates the travel distance between the citizen\textquotesingle{}s home and their job, chooses a strategy based on the distance, and transitions them to the \mbox{\hyperlink{classAtWorkState}{At\+Work\+State}}.

\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
