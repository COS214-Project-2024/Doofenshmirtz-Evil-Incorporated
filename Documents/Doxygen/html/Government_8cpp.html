Detailed Description

Implements the Government class for creating Government objects. Government acts as a central command for interacting with the city. It forms the Subject for the Observer Design Pattern, the Invoker for the Command Design Pattern, and is used by the Facade Implements methods such as: Collecting tax, Collecting resources, executeCommand (for the different commands), and notifies the city to update.
