Detailed Description

Represents a residential building in the city.

The Residential class is a derived class from the Building base class. It encapsulates the properties and behaviors specific to residential buildings, including capacity management and resource collection.

Constructs a Residential building and fills it with citizens.

Initializes the residential unit with a random capacity between 10 and 109, sets the used capacity to the total capacity, and populates the resident vector with dynamically allocated Citizen objects.
