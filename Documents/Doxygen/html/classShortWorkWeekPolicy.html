Detailed Description

Command to implement a short workweek policy in a CityUnit.

This policy allows for the enhancement of the workweek structure by deducting a certain amount from the balance and increasing the short week policy multiplier.

Executes the command to enhance the short workweek policy.

Deducts a specific amount from the balance and updates the short week multiplier if there are sufficient funds.

If the balance is sufficient, the policy deducts a fixed amount from the balance and updates the short week multiplier of the associated CityUnit.
