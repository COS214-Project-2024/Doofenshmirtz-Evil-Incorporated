Detailed Description

Concrete state representing a citizen's location when they are at home.

This class defines the behavior of a citizen when they are in their home state. It inherits from CitizenLocationState and provides a specific implementation for the travel function.

void AtHomeState::travel ( Citizen *  citizen)
virtual

Handles the travel logic for a citizen in the AtHomeState.

Transitions the citizen from the AtHomeState to the AtWorkState, if the citizen has a job. If the citizen is unemployed, they remain in the AtHomeState.

Parameters
citizenThe citizen who is attempting to travel.

This method transitions a citizen from their home to their workplace, provided they are employed (i.e., they have a job). The method calculates the travel distance between the citizen's home and their job, chooses a strategy based on the distance, and transitions them to the AtWorkState.

Parameters
citizenThe citizen attempting to travel from home.
Note
If the citizen is unemployed (i.e., they don't have a job), they remain in the AtHomeState, and no travel occurs.

Implements CitizenLocationState.
